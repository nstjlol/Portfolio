About Me:

My name is Nicholas St-Jacques, I'm a 22-year-old technology enthusiast with a deep passion for tinkering with cutting-edge tech. My love for technology can be traced back to my childhood when I spent countless hours playing video games. This hobby ignited my curiosity and propelled me to explore the world of modding to enhance my gaming experience. Through this exploration, I ventured into programming and began learning languages such as Lua and Python.

Determined to further develop my technical skills, I enrolled at Cambrian College to study as a Network Technician. Upon completion, I had not only acquired essential networking knowledge but also expanded my abilities in problem-solving and critical thinking. My insatiable thirst for knowledge led me to pursue further education Cambrian, this time focusing on Computer Programming and Internet of Things (IoT).

During my time at Cambrian College, I have had the opportunity to work on several innovative IoT projects, allowing me to apply my programming skills to real-world applications. This hands-on experience, coupled with my strong educational background, has provided me with a solid foundation to tackle future technological challenges.

As I continue on this exciting journey, I eagerly look forward to connecting with like-minded individuals, sharing ideas, and pushing the boundaries of what technology can accomplish. Together, let's shape the world of tomorrow!
